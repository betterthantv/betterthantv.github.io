better than TV
some jazz from Cambridge UK

 better than TV plays at events and pubs and it is great for your party.

We perform regularly at the main jazz events at the University of Cambridge: twice at Clare Jazz, as support to JJ Wheeler quintet and Led Bib, and twice at Jazz at John's, supporting Ant Law and the Preston-Glasgow-Lowe trio.

Listen to some of our live performances on YouTube!
